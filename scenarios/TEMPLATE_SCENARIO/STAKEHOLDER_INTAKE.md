## Discovery Guidance

Good learner questions include:

- What problem are you trying to solve?
- Who is affected?
- What does success look like?
- What happens today?
- Where does the current process break down?
- What should users do differently?
- What should Salesforce do automatically?
- What should remain manual?
- What reporting or visibility do you need?
- What exceptions happen in the real world?

Adjust these questions for the scenario.
